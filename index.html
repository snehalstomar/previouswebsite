About Me

Hi! I am Snehal Singh Tomar, a graduate student at IIT Madras pursuing an MS in Electrical Engineering. At IITM, I am advised by Prof. A.N. Rajagopalan. My research interests lie at the intersection of Machine Learning, Computer Vision and Robotics.

I recently graduated from Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal with a B.Tech. in Electronics and Communcation Engineering, having earned a minor specialization in Signal Processing.

During my undergrad, I completed several research internships under the guidance of Prof. Shubhendu Bhasin at IIT Delhi, working in the domain of Control Systems and Robotics. I was also a part of my university's AI Robotics Team, where we worked on building an autonomous car.
