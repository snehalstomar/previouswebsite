Python-Based Fuzzy Classifier for Cashew Kernels
Snehal S. Tomar and Narendra V.G.
Advances in Intelligent Systems and Computing, vol 816. Springer

We presented an efficient fuzzy classification algorithm for categorizing cashew kernels into their coomercial classes (having highly overlapping intervals of definition) on the basis of physical properties such as length, width, thickness etc. Our work was awarded the Best Paper Award at SocProS 2017.
